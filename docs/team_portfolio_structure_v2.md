# Team Portfolio Structure v2.0

## 团队作品集 v2.0 总结构

### 01. Cover / 团队封面页

**Organizing Space / 组织空间**
**Connecting Perception / 连接感知**
**Generating Images / 生成图景**

VIRTURA is a decentralized art collective and research network centered on perceptual migration, spatial organization, and image generation.

We work through live performance, spatial image-making, real-time systems, research experiments, and digital tools to organize structure, perception, and spatial experience into works, environments, and interfaces that can be entered.

---

### 02. Team Positioning / 团队定位

VIRTURA is not a conventional studio built only around deliverables.
It is a decentralized creative collective and research framework that connects live visuals, immersive environments, previsualization systems, digital archives, and experimental tools.

The collective works across performance, space, systems, and image organization, while maintaining a structure that supports both public-facing projects and long-term research.

---

### 05. Working Method / 工作方式

We work through a hybrid structure:

* concept and narrative framing
* image and spatial development
* real-time prototyping
* media organization and documentation
* iterative testing across venues and formats

This allows the collective to move between artistic research, commissioned work, public presentation, and experimental development without treating them as isolated fields.
